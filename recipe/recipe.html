Best Chocolate Chips Cookies

ingredients

    1/2 cup (100grams)butter
  • 1 package of large marshmallows
  • 5 cups (about 500 grams)crispy rice cereal

Directions

  1. Melt the butter in a large sauce pan.
  2. Add the marshmallows and stir them until they've melted.
  3. Remove the sauce pan from the heat.
  4. Continue to stir for a few more minutes.
  5. Add the crispy rice cereal and stir until it's coated with melted marshmallows.
  6. Spread the rice crispy treats evenly in a pan and let them cool.
  7. Cut the treats into squares.
